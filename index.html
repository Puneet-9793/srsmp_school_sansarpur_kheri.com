Welcome to SRSMPS Sansarpur Kheri

We would like waemly welcome you to my school SRSMP School Sansarpr Kheri. Choosing your School is such an important decision. Your child has already started on a learning journey and we see our role as being partners with you in steering your child alone that journey Sri Rajendra Singh Memorial Public School is a unique school exclusively for your child, an outcome of the deep aspirations, values and expertise of the Director, Mr. Manjeet Singh. Our school offers an idyllic and conducive environment for 'Sarva Gun Sampanna' - (holistic development) for its students, enabling them to nurture their telents and emerge out as independent, confident and virtuous students. The school proudly upholds its commitments to all round excellence and emphasizes on its motto and believes in educating the students in an atmosphere of spirituality. Following these principles, our School has given utmost importance to values in its curriculum. This unique strength enables our students to face challenges of life. As teachers it is important for us to make our students eager learners wanting to know every thing that is around them.
